About SoftAn

Welcome to the Society of Food Scientists and Nutritionists (SOFTAN)! Established with a firm commitment to nurturing the academic and professional growth of students, SOFTAN is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering excellence, social responsibility, integrity, and competitiveness in the fields of food science and nutrition.

What we recognise

At SOFTAN, we recognize the paramount importance of academic excellence and the pursuit of knowledge in the ever-evolving domains of food science and nutrition. Our organization is designed to provide students with a supportive environment where they can engage in enriching academic activities, exchange ideas, and enhance their understanding of the intricate dynamics within the field.

Emphasis

SOFTAN places a strong emphasis on social welfare and community engagement. We believe in the power of collective action to address societal challenges and promote positive change. Through various outreach initiatives and collaborative projects, our members actively contribute to the betterment of society and advocate for sustainable practices in food science and nutrition.
